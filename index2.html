OUR MISSION

Etz Chaim and Aish Philadelphia have entered into a new collaboration! Both organizations have a rich, decades-long history of engaging the greater community in Jewish education and engagement. Beginning on January 1, 2019, these two entities began to work together as one unified entity — Aish Chaim.

Combining the best of both organizations, Aish Chaim is a platform for every Jewish person in Greater Philadelphia to find their place among ‘The Tribe’. With a rich blend of learning and engagement programs, as well as our spirit-filled Shabbat services with optional learning groups, Aish Chaim is truly welcoming for all those who choose to join in.

We hope to see you soon at one of our Shabbat service and learning programs!
